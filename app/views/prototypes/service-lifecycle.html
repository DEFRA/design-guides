Service Lifecycle

Who is the service lifecycle for?

The service lifecycle is guidance for people who own, deliver or work on digital services that strive to meet the needs of users and outcomes of our organisation.

Understand

Understand the problem

Understand the actual problem trying to be solved.

Deliver

Agile delivery

Use agile delivery methods to create a service which is simple to use and intuitive enough that users succeed the first time.

Improve

Making services better

Continously improve and measure the success of a service to ensure it is meeting standards.

Retire

End of service

What to do if a product or service is no longer delivering organisational outcomes.
