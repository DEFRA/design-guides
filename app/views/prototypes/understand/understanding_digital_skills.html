What we mean by digital skills

The digital, data and technology professions job roles and descriptions can be found on GOV.UK.

The activities and outcomes

The team will create the best work when they:

  • Are stable, members are committed to the team for a sustained period and have regular patterns of engagement
  • Have built trust with each other and find way to retain this trust
  • Are empowered to make decisions about the work that it does day to day
  • Have an alignment to a clear vision and goals
  • Communicate daily and where possible are co-located
  • Are non hierarchical, every team member has an important role to play and leadership comes from multiple places within the team

What is required

A multidisciplinary team should have all the roles it needs to design, build and operate a service. They are able to deliver at pace and shouldn’t be blocked from delivering by missing skills or by having too many external dependencies on other teams.

Ideally people should only be a member of one agile team at a time, and should fulfil one role within the team. Where this isn’t possible careful consideration should be given to how best to share expertise across teams, and Product Owners and Delivery Managers should be consulted before any decision is made.
